The Ducasse de Mons or Doudou is a popular festival in the city of Mons. With origins in the Middle Ages, every year it brings together thousands of participants in the city. In 2005 it was recognized by UNESCO as an example of the Intangible Heritage of Humanity. Doudou is the highpoint of the year for the city of Mons!

Through the ‘Dou do it’ exploration, you can relive the magic of Doudou!
